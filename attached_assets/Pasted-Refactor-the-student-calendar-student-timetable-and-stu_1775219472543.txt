Refactor the /student/calendar, /student/timetable, and /student/leave modules into a high-performance, synchronized operations hub. Use a Modern Emerald Green (#10b981) and White professional theme, optimized for Motorola 60 Fusion and Desktop.

1. THE SCHOOL CALENDAR (/student/calendar):

Visual Month View: A high-end, interactive calendar strictly filtered by the student's school_id.

Event Legend:

Emerald Green: School Events (Sports, Fest).

Soft Red: Holidays & Sundays (School Closed).

Royal Blue: Examination Dates.

Interaction: Tapping a date opens a Glassmorphism Modal showing Event Title, Venue, and Description.

2. THE SMART TIMETABLE (/student/timetable):

Weekly Navigation: A horizontal 'Day Strip' (Mon to Sat) at the top.

Daily Feed: A vertical list of periods mapped to the student's class_id and section_id.

Dynamic Logic:

Live Highlight: Use a pulsing Emerald border for the 'Active Period' based on the system clock.

Holiday Sync: If the Calendar marks a date as a 'Holiday', display a professional 'School Closed' illustration instead of the timetable for that day.

3. THE MULTI-LEVEL LEAVE HUB (/student/leave):

Student Dashboard: Top cards showing 'Total Leaves' and 'Current Status'.

The Request Form (Drawer):

Fields: Category, Start/End Date, Reason, and Attachment.

Smart Routing: Attach the student's class_id and section_id to route the request directly to the Class Teacher's Dashboard.

Teacher-Side Approval (/teacher/dashboard/leave):

Filter: Teacher only sees their own class's requests.

Actions:

Approve: Mark as 'Approved' (Updates Student Attendance automatically).

Reject: Add a rejection reason.

Escalate: Move the request to the Principal's Dashboard for final review.

4. UI/UX & Technical Guardrails:

Strict Multi-Tenancy: Every fetch request must include WHERE school_id = $1.

Format Standards: All dates must be in dd/mm/yyyy format.

Mobile UX: Full support for touch-swipe gestures to change months or days.

Icons: Use Lucide-React (CalendarDays, Clock, ShieldCheck).
